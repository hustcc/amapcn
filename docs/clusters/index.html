Clusters

Visualize large datasets with automatic point clustering.

The MapClusterLayercomponent uses AMap's built-in clustering to efficiently render large numbers of points. Points are automatically grouped into clusters at low zoom levels, and expand as you zoom in.

Basic Example

Click on clusters to zoom in. Click individual points to see details in a popup.
